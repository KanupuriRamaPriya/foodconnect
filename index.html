How It Works

A seamless connection from surplus to sustenance in three simple steps.

1

Post Excess Food

Restaurants, events, or individuals list surplus food on the app with a few details.

2

Match & Notify

Nearby NGOs and volunteers are instantly notified of available, safe-to-eat food.

3

Pickup & Distribute

Volunteers quickly collect and deliver the food to shelters before it expires.
